FarSpace832c

This game is a fast FPS and will teach you some things about spatial exploration. You will conquer the exoplanet Gliese 832c as a robot sent by humans. Secure the planet before humans travel to settle in.

This project has been made by a small team of students in their second year in Junia ISEN in Lille.
