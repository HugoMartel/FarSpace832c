project.exe

Our goal for this project is to allow the player to learn about space exploration and space colonisation. We decided to create a fast fps combined with a mangement game. The fast fps will be about killing alien on the new planet where you land. And the management will be the part where you create your own base and you upragde it. By doing so we hope players would be able to learn what's needed on a new planet to prepare a human colonisation.
